About Our Ghibli Style Converter

Our Ghibli Style Converter is powered by advanced AI technology that has been trained on thousands of frames from Studio Ghibli films to capture the distinctive artistic style that has enchanted audiences worldwide.

We've developed this tool out of our love for Hayao Miyazaki's unique visual storytelling and to give everyone the opportunity to see their own photos transformed with that same magical quality.

Whether you're a die-hard Ghibli fan or simply appreciate beautiful artwork, our converter offers a fun and easy way to experience your photos in a new light.

Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported?

+

Our converter supports JPG and PNG image formats. For best results, we recommend using clear, well-lit images with good resolution.

Is there a limit to how many images I can convert?

+

Currently, we offer unlimited conversions for all users. However, we recommend spacing out multiple conversions to ensure optimal processing speed.

How long does the conversion process take?

+

Most images are processed within 15-30 seconds, depending on the complexity of the image and current server load. Larger or more detailed images may take slightly longer.

Are my images stored on your servers?

+

We prioritize your privacy. Your original images and the converted results are temporarily processed on our secure servers but are automatically deleted after processing. We do not permanently store or use your images for any purpose.

Can I use the converted images commercially?

+

The converted images are for personal use only. Commercial use may require additional licensing due to the artistic style being derived from copyrighted works. Please consult with a legal professional for specific use cases.
